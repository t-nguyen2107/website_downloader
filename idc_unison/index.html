Welcome

Welcome to the UnisonSteadfast Independence Day Conference - one of the most highly acclaimed conferences for international insurance professionals worldwide, which, by our tradition, takes place in different countries and cities around the globe. Every year, our event attracts associates from our members and selected business partners, representing over 140 countries.

The conference offers a high-profile program that influences strategic decisions among the world’s top independent brokers, as well as unique opportunities to network with decision-makers from more than 700 brokerage firms, producing over US$ 40 billion in premium.

The upcoming IDC takes on special significance as we prepare to celebrate a momentous milestone: In 2025, we are celebrating our 20th Anniversary and our valued members and partners around the world who have continuously contributed to the success of our global organization.  

We are excited to see you in Munich!
